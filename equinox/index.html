Equinox is a raiding and social guild on Ragnaros, Molten WoW which is focused primarily on doing endgame raids.
Our raid times are around 9PM Server Time/8PM Server Time on Sundays.
If you'd like to join make an application and it will be reviewed by our staff and given response to as fast as possible.
This guild was made out of nothing to achieve a community which gives opportunity to players enjoying World of Warcraft
to experience and raid end-game content, so anyone who finds themselves in this group is welcome.
